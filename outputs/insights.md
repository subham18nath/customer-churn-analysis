# Churn Analysis - Key Insights

**Overall churn rate (baseline):** 30.0%

## Headline insight

Customers on **Month-to-month** contracts with **Fiber optic** internet and **TechSupport = No** churn at **66.4%** - about **2.2x** the baseline.

## Top 5 high-risk segments

| Contract | InternetService | TechSupport | churn_rate | customers | risk_multiple |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Month-to-month | Fiber optic | No | 0.664 | 997 | 2.21 |
| Month-to-month | Fiber optic | Yes | 0.629 | 660 | 2.093 |
| Month-to-month | DSL | No | 0.455 | 806 | 1.516 |
| Month-to-month | DSL | Yes | 0.402 | 498 | 1.337 |
| One year | Fiber optic | No | 0.312 | 397 | 1.04 |

## Revenue at stake (top segment)

- Annual revenue exposed: **$956,349**
- Revenue saved with a 25% churn reduction: **$158,652**

## Retention strategy

1. Migrate month-to-month customers to annual contracts with a discount.
2. Launch a 90-day onboarding journey to protect first-year customers.
3. Bundle a free tech-support trial for at-risk fiber customers.
4. Incentivize auto-pay to move users off electronic check.
5. Proactive quality outreach for high-paying fiber customers.
